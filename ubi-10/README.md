`https://<che_fqdn>/#<url_to_a_sample_project>?image=<ubi10_based_image_for_testing>&editor-image=<url_to_a_the_editor_image>`. <br />

- https://eclipse.dev/che/docs/stable/end-user-guide/url-parameter-for-container-image/
